Privacy Policy for Twitch Auto Refresh Plus

Effective Date: 03/11/2024

Introduction

Twitch Auto Refresh Plus is a browser extension designed to automatically refresh Twitch.tv livestreams when they encounter connection issues or playback problems, ensuring uninterrupted viewing experience.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. It operates entirely on the user's device and does not transmit any data to external servers. The only data stored locally are your extension preferences (using Chrome's storage API) which never leave your device.

Data Usage

The extension only accesses and monitors Twitch.tv stream pages to detect connection issues. All processing happens locally on your device. Your preferences (such as refresh intervals and notification settings) are stored locally and are only used to customize the extension's behavior.

Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties. The extension operates independently and does not communicate with any external servers.

Permissions

The extension requires specific permissions to function:

  • Access to Twitch.tv websites: To monitor stream status and perform refreshes
  • Storage: To save your preferences locally
  • Tabs: To refresh tabs when needed
  • Active Tab: To interact with the current stream tab

User Rights

Since no personal data is collected or stored externally, there are no data-related rights to exercise. You can manage your local preferences through the extension's settings panel, and you can uninstall the extension at any time to remove all local data.

Contact Information
